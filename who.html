Carter Evins

❝ Anti-racism is essential because it promotes a society where everyone is treated with dignity and respect, regardless of their racial background. By actively challenging and dismantling systemic racism, anti-racism works towards creating a more just and inclusive society where opportunities are not limited by one's race or ethnicity. Anti-racism fosters empathy, understanding, and unity among diverse racial and ethnic groups, promoting a sense of belonging and community for all individuals. ❞
