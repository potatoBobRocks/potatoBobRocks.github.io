Why We Do This

Equality?

At The New Heirarchy, we support equality in most fashions. However, we are not communist, and perfect equality is not possible. However, there is still a lot of work to do and we are on track for success.

Order?

At The New Heirarchy, we believe that a well-defined social heirarchy is important for success. We demand that the heirarchy is equal and there is no favoritism based upon race.

Capitalism?

At The New Heirarchy, we are more capitalist than anyone you know. We believe in little government interference with social structure and economics, in order to prevent communism.
